About Me

Hello, my name is Blase Standen and I am a computer scientist graduate from the University of Hull who focuses on Virtual Reality and Games Development. I am currently working as a VR/AR developer intern at The Aura Innovation Center. I am a motivated, diligent, conscientious individual who is keen to develop my experiences and skills in software development.

Team working is an important component of my development and this has enabled a productive, collaborative approach to designing solutions to the tasks that I and my colleagues have been set. I look to further develop my skills in a variety of languages including C#.

I enjoy developing new solutions to the challenges which are presented in software operation and development. I am keen to understand the application of industry standards for coding practice and how these can be met.

I have a friendly, communicative, organised and professional approach to my work and enjoy exceeding expectations. This is essential when customers rely on good delivery to agreed deadlines. I have worked in roles where this is important when speaking with and helping customers be this face to face or on the telephone and as part of a wider team where multitasking and adaptability is necessary.
