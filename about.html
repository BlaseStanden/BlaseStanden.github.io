About Me

During my experiences as a software developer, I have worked at a number of software development companies developing virtual reality software for businesses to use in areas such as training, advertising, and education. I am a motivated, diligent, conscientious individual who is keen to develop my experiences and skills in technology.

I am an excellent conversationalist and love working in a team environment but I can also work independently and take the lead when required. I am adept at problem-solving and finding innovative solutions to the delivery of practical requirements.

Team working is an important component of experiences and this has enabled a productive, collaborative approach to design solutions to the tasks that I and my colleagues have been set. I look to further develop my skills in agile development, website development and a variety of languages including JavaScript, HTML and CSS.

I enjoy developing new solutions to the challenges which are presented in software operation and development. I am keen to understand the application of industry standards for coding practice and how these can be met.

I have a friendly, communicative, organised and professional approach to my work and enjoy exceeding expectations. This is essential when customers rely on good delivery to agreed deadlines. I have worked in roles where this is important when speaking with and helping customers be this face to face or on the telephone and as part of a wider team where multitasking and adaptability are necessary.
